Cara membuat QR uji (dynamic — nominal tertanam) 
curl -X POST http://localhost:{{ selectedSim?.port }}/v1.0/qr/qr-mpm-generate \
  -H "X-PARTNER-ID: PARTNER001" -H "X-CALLBACK-URL: http://localhost:8080/api/admin/v1/webhook-sink" \
  -d '{{ '{' }}"partnerReferenceNo":"PR-001","merchantId":"MERCHANT01",
       "amount":{{ '{' }}"value":"25000.00","currency":"IDR"{{ '}' }}{{ '}' }}'
Cara membuat QR uji (static — tanpa nominal)

Cara refund (setelah QR dibayar) 
curl -X POST http://localhost:{{ selectedSim?.port }}/v1.0/qr/qr-mpm-refund \
  -H "X-PARTNER-ID: PARTNER001" \
  -d '{{ '{' }}"originalReferenceNo":"<referenceNo>","partnerRefundNo":"RF-001",
       "refundAmount":{{ '{' }}"value":"25000.00","currency":"IDR"{{ '}' }}{{ '}' }}'
